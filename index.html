Azerbaijan State Agricultural University (ADAU) and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Azerbaijan State Agricultural University (ADAU) is recognized as one of the leading educational institutions in the country and region, thanks to its commitment to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). ADAU plays a significant role in the development of Azerbaijan's agricultural sector, taking important steps to ensure the country's sustainable future. The strategic goal of the university is not only to advance agricultural education but also to support scientific research activities that ensure sustainable development in the agricultural sector. ADAU's educational programs and research activities are aligned with the global Sustainable Development Goals. By equipping its students with knowledge and skills that allow them to compete on a global level, the university also instills the principles of sustainability in them. This ensures that ADAU's students grow into global leaders who are sensitive to global issues and committed to sustainability principles. ADAU applies innovative approaches to ensure sustainable development in the agricultural and agrarian sectors and promotes the use of environmentally friendly technologies.
Among the most significant steps taken by the university is the efficient management of water resources in the agricultural sector. ADAU promotes the application of modern technologies for water conservation and its efficient use, and conducts scientific research in this direction. The preservation of water resources is of great importance for increasing agricultural productivity and protecting the environment. ADAU collaborates with many international organizations in this field, contributing to the sustainable development of the agricultural sector both regionally and globally.
The preservation of biodiversity is also one of ADAU's priority areas. The university implements several important projects in the field of protecting biological diversity in agriculture and the efficient use of natural resources. ADAU's scientists and students conduct scientific research in this direction, working on the protection of ecosystems and the development of sustainable agricultural practices. These projects are of great importance both locally and internationally and contribute to the university's recognition on a global scale.
ADAU's commitment to the Sustainable Development Goals is also reflected in the projects it carries out in cooperation with international organizations. ADAU closely collaborates with various universities, research centers, and international organizations around the world. These collaborations facilitate the implementation of joint research and projects aimed at ensuring the sustainable development of the agricultural and agrarian sectors. ADAU's international recognition and achievements significantly contribute to the development of the agricultural sector in line with Azerbaijan's national development strategy.
In the university's forward-looking strategy, the implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals is one of the main priorities. ADAU mobilizes its students and community to achieve these goals, ensuring their commitment to sustainability principles. This highlights the university's prominence not only in education and research activities but also in the field of social responsibility. ADAU continues its efforts to ensure that future generations live in a greener, safer, and more sustainable world. As a result, Azerbaijan State Agricultural University makes significant contributions to the sustainable development of the agricultural sector in the country and the region, thanks to its commitment to the Sustainable Development Goals and the important steps it has taken in this direction. The university's strategic goals are aimed at achieving significant results in the development of agricultural education and research, ecological sustainability, biodiversity conservation, and the efficient management of water resources. By ensuring that future generations are raised based on the principles of sustainable development, ADAU leads the development of the agricultural sector in line with Azerbaijan's national development strategy.

News and events

On November 13, 1920, by the decision signed by the Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of Azerbaijan, Nariman Narimanov, 5 faculties were established under the Baku Polytechnic Institute: oil industry, electromechanics, engineering-construction, agriculture, and economics. With the creation of the agricultural faculty, the training of the first higher agricultural specialists in our country began. The first graduation of specialists took place in 1924.
